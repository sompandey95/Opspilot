# ShopEasy Cancellation Policy

**Effective Date: June 1, 2026**
_Applies to all orders placed on or after June 1, 2026._

---

## 1. Cancellation Before Shipment

### 1.1 Customer-Initiated Cancellation
You may cancel any order free of charge as long as its status has not moved to **"Dispatched"**. This includes orders in "Confirmed" and "Packed" status.

**How to cancel:**
1. Go to **My Orders** → select the order → tap **Cancel Order**.
2. Select a cancellation reason.
3. Confirm cancellation.

Cancellation is processed instantly and a full refund is initiated immediately:

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| UPI | 24–48 hours |
| Credit / Debit Card | 5–7 business days |
| Net Banking | 5–7 business days |
| ShopEasy Wallet | Instant |
| COD | No charge |

### 1.2 COD Orders Cancelled Before Shipment
No money has been collected, so no refund is applicable. The order is simply closed.

---

## 2. Cancellation After Shipment

Once an order is **"Dispatched"**, in-app cancellation is disabled. Your options are:

### 2.1 Refuse Delivery
When the delivery executive arrives, refuse to accept the package. Clearly state you are refusing it. The package is marked "Delivery Refused" and returned to origin (RTO).

- **Prepaid orders**: Full refund processed once RTO is confirmed (typically 5–10 business days after refusal, depending on courier return transit time).
- **COD orders**: No charge. The ₹30 COD handling fee is non-refundable.

### 2.2 Do Not Accept and File Return
If you accepted the delivery in error, initiate a return within the return window. A ₹75 restocking fee applies for change-of-mind returns on items above ₹999 (see Refund Policy Section 3.4).

---

## 3. Cancellation by Seller

If a seller cancels your order after you have placed and paid for it (due to stock unavailability, pricing error, or other seller-side issues):

- You receive a **100% refund** of the amount paid.
- You also receive a **₹100 compensation coupon** valid for 60 days from cancellation, applicable on your next order of ₹500 or more.
- Refund timelines match those in Section 1.1 above.

Repeated seller cancellations are tracked and sellers with a cancellation rate above 2% face penalties under ShopEasy's Seller Performance Policy.

---

## 4. Cancellation Limits per Account

To maintain platform integrity and prevent abuse, the following limits apply:

| Period | Cancellation Limit |
|---|---|
| Calendar month | 5 cancellations |
| Rolling 90 days | 10 cancellations |

**On reaching the limit:**
- The 5th/10th cancellation in the period is allowed, but a warning is sent to your registered email.
- Subsequent cancellation attempts trigger an **account review**.
- During an account review (typically 2–3 business days), cancellation access may be temporarily restricted. Existing orders can still be delivered or returned normally.
- If the review finds no genuine cause for high cancellation rate (e.g., no pattern of problematic orders), your account remains in good standing but the limit resets only at the start of the next month.

Cancellations due to seller-initiated cancellations or ShopEasy system errors do **not** count towards your cancellation limit.

---

## 5. Cancellation of Prepaid vs COD Orders

- **Prepaid orders**: Refund is issued to the original payment method (see timelines in Section 1.1).
- **COD orders**: No refund applies for pre-shipment cancellations (no money collected). For post-shipment COD orders that are refused, the COD handling fee (₹30) is non-refundable.

---

## 6. Bulk / Wholesale Order Cancellations

For orders above ₹25,000 or orders identified as bulk/commercial in nature, cancellations must be requested via email to **b2b@shopeasy.in**. Standard cancellation timelines may be extended by 2–3 business days for review.
