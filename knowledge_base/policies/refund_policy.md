# ShopEasy Refund Policy

**Effective Date: June 1, 2026**
_Applies to all orders placed on or after June 1, 2026. For orders placed before this date, the policy in effect at time of purchase applies._

---

## 1. Eligibility

A refund may be requested when:
- The item is returned within the applicable return window (10 days for most categories; 7 days for electronics from the date of delivery).
- The return reason is supported under this policy (see Section 3).
- The item is in the condition required under Section 4.

Refunds are not available for:
- Items returned after the return window has closed.
- Items in non-returnable categories (Section 5).
- Items that fail quality inspection on return.

## 3. Refund Amounts by Return Reason

### 3.1 Defective or Damaged Product
Refund: **100% of order value** (item price + original shipping charges if any).
Evidence required: Photos/video of defect uploaded at time of return initiation.

### 3.2 Wrong Item Received
Refund: **100% of order value** + a **₹50 courtesy coupon** valid for 30 days.
ShopEasy arranges pickup at no charge.

### 3.3 Item Not as Described
Refund: **100% of order value** (item price + original shipping charges).
"Not as described" means a material difference from the product listing (colour, size, specifications). Minor variations in shade are not eligible.

### 3.4 Change of Mind
- Items priced **≤ ₹999**: **100% refund** (no restocking fee).
- Items priced **> ₹999**: **Full refund minus ₹75 restocking fee** per item.
- Original shipping charges are **not refunded** for change-of-mind returns.
- Return shipping is the customer's responsibility unless a pickup is available in your pincode (pickup availability shown during return initiation).

### 3.5 Seller Cancellation
If a seller cancels your order after payment: **100% refund + ₹100 compensation coupon** valid for 60 days.

---

## 4. Item Condition Requirements for Return

To be eligible for a refund, the returned item must:
- Be unused and in its original condition.
- Include all original packaging, accessories, manuals, warranty cards, and free gifts received.
- Have original price tags and brand labels intact.
- For electronics: IMEI/serial number must match the invoice.
- Not show signs of damage caused after delivery (customer-side damage voids refund eligibility).

---

## 5. Non-Refundable Categories

The following are **not eligible for return or refund** under any circumstances:

- Innerwear, lingerie, swimwear, socks (hygiene)
- Customised / personalised products (monogrammed, engraved, custom-printed)
- Perishable goods (fresh food, flowers, cakes)
- Opened software, digital downloads, or gift cards once redeemed
- Consumables (health supplements, cosmetics) if the seal is broken
- Any item explicitly marked "Non-Returnable" on its product page

**Exception**: Non-returnable items may be refunded if received in a defective or damaged condition, subject to photographic evidence verified by ShopEasy.

## 7. COD Order Refunds

For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds are processed as follows:
1. During return initiation, choose either **Bank Account Transfer (NEFT)** or **ShopEasy Wallet**.
2. For bank transfer: provide your account number and IFSC code. ShopEasy does not store bank details beyond the refund transaction.
3. For ShopEasy Wallet: the amount is credited instantly upon verification and can be used for future purchases or transferred to a linked bank account.

The ₹30 COD handling fee is **non-refundable** except in cases of seller cancellation or defective item.

---

## 8. Partial Deliveries

If your order contained multiple items and only some were delivered:
- Undelivered items are automatically refunded within 48 hours of the delivery window closing.
- If some items in a combo/bundle are missing, the proportional value of missing items is refunded.
