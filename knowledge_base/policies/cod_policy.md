# ShopEasy Cash on Delivery (COD) Policy

**Effective Date: June 1, 2026**
_Applies to all COD orders placed on or after June 1, 2026._

---

## 1. COD Availability Rules

Cash on Delivery is a payment option subject to the following eligibility conditions. All conditions must be satisfied simultaneously.

### 1.1 Order Value
- COD is available **only on orders with a total cart value of ₹5,000 or less** (inclusive of all items, shipping charges, and COD fee).
- Orders exceeding ₹5,000 at checkout are automatically excluded from the COD option.

### 1.2 Delivery Pincode
- COD is available only in **COD-serviceable pincodes**. Not all pincodes that accept standard delivery support COD.
- Serviceability is checked automatically at checkout. If COD is unavailable for your pincode, only prepaid options are shown.
- Remote pincodes served exclusively by India Post are typically **not COD-eligible** due to logistical constraints.

### 1.3 Product Category
The following categories are **not eligible for COD** regardless of order value or pincode:
- Smartphones and tablets priced above ₹15,000 (individual item price)
- Laptops and personal computers
- Gold, silver, and diamond jewellery
- Bullion, coins, and precious metals
- Items sold by select international sellers
- Any item explicitly marked "COD Not Available" on its product page

### 1.4 Account Standing
Customers with a **COD return/refusal rate above 30%** over the past 90 days may temporarily lose COD access. A notification is sent to the registered email/mobile when this restriction is applied. COD access is restored once the rate falls below 30% over a subsequent 90-day window or after a review by the Trust & Safety team.

---

## 2. COD Handling Fee

A **₹30 COD handling fee** is charged per order (not per item). This fee:
- Is shown separately at checkout before you confirm the order.
- Is **non-refundable** in most scenarios, including change-of-mind returns and post-dispatch refusals.
- **Is refunded** if the seller cancels the order, if the item is defective, or if ShopEasy cancels the order on its own initiative.

---

## 3. COD Payment at Doorstep

### 3.1 Payment Methods Accepted
- **Cash**: Exact change is strongly preferred. Delivery executives carry limited change (typically up to ₹500). If exact change is unavailable on either side, the delivery may need to be rescheduled.
- **UPI at doorstep**: Many ShopEasy delivery partners accept UPI payment via a QR code shown on the delivery executive's device. This is subject to the courier partner's capability and may not be available in all areas.

### 3.2 What to Keep Ready
- The full COD amount (item price + ₹30 COD fee, as shown in your order summary).
- Your delivery OTP (if applicable for orders above ₹2,000 — see Delivery FAQ).
- A valid photo ID if the delivery executive requests it for high-value orders.

### 3.3 No Post-Delivery Payment
Once the package is accepted and the delivery is marked complete, no further payment is required or collected. Be wary of anyone claiming to collect additional payment after delivery — report such incidents to support immediately.

---

## 4. COD Refunds

Since COD orders are paid in cash, refunds cannot be reversed in cash. Refunds for COD orders are processed through:

### 4.1 Bank Account Transfer (NEFT)
- You provide your **bank account number and IFSC code** when initiating the return.
- The refund is transferred via NEFT within **5–7 business days** after the returned item is verified.
- ShopEasy does not retain bank account details after the refund is processed.
- The beneficiary name on the bank account must match the name on your ShopEasy account.

### 4.2 ShopEasy Wallet
- Amount is credited to your ShopEasy Wallet **instantly** upon return verification.
- Wallet balance can be used for future purchases on ShopEasy.
- You can transfer Wallet balance to any linked bank account within 3–5 business days (minimum transfer: ₹100).

### 4.3 Refund for Cancelled COD Orders
- If a COD order is cancelled before dispatch, no money has been collected, so no refund is required.
- The ₹30 COD fee is only collected at delivery; if the order is cancelled before delivery, no COD fee is charged.

---

## 5. COD Order Tracking and Confirmation

COD orders are confirmed immediately after placement (unlike some platforms that require a call confirmation). You will receive:
- An order confirmation SMS/WhatsApp with your Order ID.
- Standard tracking updates as the order progresses.
- A "Out for Delivery" notification on the day of delivery attempt.

---

## 6. Misuse of COD

ShopEasy monitors COD usage to prevent fraud and platform abuse. Actions that may result in COD suspension or account action include:
- Repeatedly refusing or returning COD orders without a valid reason.
- Providing false delivery addresses for COD orders.
- Placing the same order multiple times via COD and cancelling all but one.
