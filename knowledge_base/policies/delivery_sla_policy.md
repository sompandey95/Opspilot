# ShopEasy Delivery SLA Policy

**Effective Date: May 15, 2026**
_Applies to all orders dispatched on or after May 15, 2026._

---

## 1. Delivery SLA by Zone

All delivery timelines are measured in **business days** from the date of dispatch (not order placement). Business days exclude Sundays and gazetted public holidays.

### 1.1 Zone Definitions and SLAs

| Zone | Coverage | Standard SLA | Express SLA |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Metro** | Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad | 3–5 business days | 1–2 business days |
| **Tier-2** | Jaipur, Lucknow, Nagpur, Indore, Bhopal, Coimbatore, Surat, Visakhapatnam, Vadodara, Patna, and 200+ cities | 5–7 business days | 2–3 business days |
| **Tier-3 / Rural** | All other serviceable pincodes via Delhivery, DTDC, or India Post | 7–12 business days | Not available |

### 1.2 Seller Dispatch SLA

ShopEasy-fulfilled items: Dispatched within **1 business day** of order placement.
Marketplace seller items: Dispatched within **2 business days** of order placement (seller SLA). Dispatch delays by sellers are tracked and penalised under Seller Performance Policy.

Total estimated delivery = Dispatch SLA + Zone delivery SLA. Estimated dates are shown at checkout and in order confirmations.

## 2. Delayed Delivery Compensation

A delivery is considered **delayed** when it exceeds the upper bound of the applicable SLA window (e.g., more than 5 business days for Metro zone standard shipping).

### 2.1 Moderate Delay (3–7 business days beyond SLA)
- Customer is eligible for a **₹100 ShopEasy coupon** credited to the account.
- Coupon is valid for 90 days and applicable on any order of ₹299 or more.
- The coupon is issued **automatically** once the system detects the delay; no customer action required.
- If not received within 24 hours of the delay being detected, contact support.

### 2.2 Severe Delay (more than 7 business days beyond SLA)
- Customer may choose either:
  a. **Wait for delivery** and receive the ₹100 coupon (see 2.1), or
  b. **Full refund** of the prepaid order amount, processed immediately upon request without requiring the order to be returned.
- COD orders that are severely delayed: no charge is applied when the order eventually arrives (customer may accept or refuse it at no cost).
- To claim a full refund for severe delay: go to **My Orders → Report Problem → Delivery Delayed** and select "Request Full Refund".

### 2.3 Express Delivery SLA Breach
If an order placed under ShopEasy Express is not delivered within the express SLA window:
- The **₹99 express fee is refunded automatically** within 24 hours of the breach being detected.
- If additionally delayed beyond the standard SLA, the moderate or severe delay compensation in 2.1/2.2 also applies.

## 3. Force Majeure Exceptions

ShopEasy's delivery SLAs and delay compensation are **not applicable** in the following circumstances:

- **Natural disasters**: floods, cyclones, earthquakes, landslides affecting courier routes (e.g., Assam/Kerala floods, Himalayan route closures).
- **Civil unrest or curfew**: bandhs, hartals, or government-declared curfews that prevent courier movement.
- **Pandemic / epidemic restrictions**: government-mandated lockdowns or zone-wise restrictions.
- **National holidays**: Deliveries may be impacted around Republic Day, Independence Day, Diwali, and Eid clusters (we proactively notify customers about likely delays).
- **Seller-side delays beyond ShopEasy's control**: If a marketplace seller fails to dispatch within their SLA, ShopEasy will pursue compensation from the seller but the force majeure exception does not apply — customer compensation under Section 2 remains applicable.

In force majeure situations, ShopEasy will:
1. Update the estimated delivery date on the order page.
2. Send proactive SMS/WhatsApp notifications.
3. Offer order cancellation with full refund if the customer does not wish to wait.

## 4. India Post Shipments

For pincodes served exclusively by India Post (remote areas, North-East states, Andaman & Nicobar Islands, Lakshadweep):
- SLA: **10–15 business days** from dispatch.
- Delay compensation thresholds are extended: moderate delay = >7 days beyond SLA, severe delay = >14 days beyond SLA.
- India Post does not support live tracking in all cases; tracking may show delayed updates.

---

## 5. Large Appliance Deliveries

For large appliances (televisions, washing machines, refrigerators, air conditioners):
- Delivery includes installation by a certified technician within **48 hours** of delivery.
- Delivery + installation SLA is scheduled separately and coordinated by ShopEasy's service partner.
- If installation is delayed beyond 48 hours, a ₹200 compensation coupon is issued.
