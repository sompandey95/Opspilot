# Seller & Marketplace — Frequently Asked Questions

_Last updated: June 1, 2026_

---

## Q1. How do I know if a product is sold by ShopEasy directly or by a third-party seller?

On every product page, you will see a **"Sold by"** label beneath the product title:
- **"Sold by ShopEasy"** or **"Fulfilled by ShopEasy"**: ShopEasy owns the inventory and is directly responsible for quality, delivery, and returns.
- **"Sold by [Seller Name]"**: A third-party seller listed on the ShopEasy marketplace. ShopEasy acts as the platform; the seller is responsible for accurate listing, packaging, and dispatch. Returns and refunds still follow ShopEasy's standard policy, but resolution may involve coordination with the seller and can take slightly longer.

Marketplace sellers are verified by ShopEasy and must meet quality standards, but ShopEasy's direct listings carry an additional **ShopEasy Quality Promise** badge.

---

## Q2. I have a dispute with a seller. How do I resolve it?

If you have a dispute (item not as described, seller refuses return, incorrect item, etc.):

1. Go to **My Orders → Contact Seller** to first attempt direct resolution. The seller has **48 hours** to respond.
2. If unresolved within 48 hours (or the seller's response is unsatisfactory), escalate by tapping **Raise Dispute with ShopEasy**.
3. ShopEasy's Seller Disputes team reviews the case within **3 business days** and may request evidence (photos, chat screenshots).
4. If found in your favour, ShopEasy will process your refund or replacement directly, then recover the amount from the seller.

ShopEasy's decision in marketplace disputes is final and binding on the seller.

---

## Q3. I received what I believe is a counterfeit or fake product. What do I do?

ShopEasy has a zero-tolerance policy for counterfeit goods. If you suspect a counterfeit:

1. **Do not use the item** — preserve it in its original condition.
2. Go to **My Orders → Report a Problem → Counterfeit / Fake Product**.
3. Upload clear photos of the item, packaging, labels, holograms, and any indicators of inauthenticity.
4. ShopEasy's Brand Protection team will contact you within **24 hours** and the seller is suspended pending investigation.
5. You will receive a **full refund immediately** without needing to return the item first (in most cases).
6. The counterfeit item may be collected by ShopEasy for inspection; do not dispose of it until contacted.

You may also report suspected counterfeits directly to the brand's Indian authorised distributor or file a complaint at the National Consumer Helpline (1800-11-4000).

## Q4. How are sellers rated on ShopEasy?

Every seller on the ShopEasy marketplace has a **Seller Score** (out of 5) visible on their seller profile page, calculated from:

| Factor | Weight |
|---|---|
| Product rating (customer reviews) | 40% |
| Dispatch time adherence | 25% |
| Return rate | 20% |
| Customer complaint rate | 15% |

Sellers with a score below **3.5** are placed on probation and receive reduced visibility. Sellers below **3.0** for two consecutive months are suspended. Customers can rate their purchase experience from **My Orders → Rate Your Experience** within 30 days of delivery.
