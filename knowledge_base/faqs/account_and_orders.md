# Account & Orders — Frequently Asked Questions

_Last updated: June 1, 2026_

---

## Q1. How do I track my order?

1. Open the ShopEasy app or website and go to **My Orders**.
2. Select the order you want to track.
3. The **Tracking** tab shows your shipment's live status with a map view (where supported by the courier).
4. You will receive automatic SMS/WhatsApp notifications at each milestone: Order Confirmed → Packed → Dispatched → Out for Delivery → Delivered.

You can also use the AWB (tracking) number displayed in your order details to track directly on the courier's website (Delhivery, BlueDart, India Post, etc.).

---

## Q2. Can I cancel my order? What refund will I get?

**Before shipment (status: "Confirmed" or "Packed"):**
- You can cancel from **My Orders → Cancel Order** at no charge.
- A full refund is processed immediately to your original payment method (UPI: 24–48 hrs; card/net banking: 5–7 business days; ShopEasy Wallet: instant).
- COD orders cancelled before shipment incur no charge.

**After shipment (status: "Dispatched" or later):**
- You cannot cancel directly. Instead, **refuse delivery** when the courier arrives — do not accept the package.
- Once the item is returned to origin (RTO), a full refund is processed for prepaid orders.
- COD orders refused at delivery incur no charge, but the COD handling fee (₹30) is non-refundable.

Customers may cancel a maximum of **5 orders per calendar month**. Exceeding this limit may trigger an account review. See the Cancellation Policy for full details.

## Q3. Can I modify my order after placing it?

Modifications are only possible while the order is in **"Confirmed"** status (before the seller packs it). You can modify:
- **Delivery address** (within the same city / serviceable pincode)
- **Quantity** (decrease only — you cannot add items to an existing order)

Once status changes to **"Packed"**, no modifications are possible. To change size, colour, or other attributes, you must cancel and re-order. To modify, go to **My Orders → Modify Order**. If the option is greyed out, the order has already been packed.

---

## Q4. Where can I find my order history?

All orders — past and present — are visible under **My Account → My Orders**. Orders are listed in reverse chronological order. You can filter by:
- Date range
- Order status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, In Transit)
- Product category

Guest checkout orders are tied to the email address used at checkout and are visible by logging in with that email or via the order confirmation email link. Guest orders are not merged into a ShopEasy account unless you explicitly create an account with the same email.

---

## Q5. Can I save multiple shipping addresses?

Yes. ShopEasy supports up to **10 saved addresses** per account. To manage them:
1. Go to **My Account → Addresses**.
2. Add, edit, or delete addresses.
3. Set one address as your **default** — it will be pre-filled at checkout.

Each address requires a valid pincode to confirm serviceability. You can also add a **preferred delivery time** note (morning / afternoon / evening) to each address, which is shared with the courier partner as a best-effort request.

---

## Q6. Can I shop without creating an account (guest checkout)?

Yes. ShopEasy supports **guest checkout** with just your mobile number or email address. As a guest:
- You can place prepaid orders (COD is not available for guest orders).
- You will receive order updates via SMS/email.
- Order history is accessible via the link in your order confirmation email.
- Returns and refunds are supported — initiate via the email link or contact support with your order ID.

Creating an account gives you access to order history, saved addresses, ShopEasy Wallet, wishlists, and personalised offers. You can convert a guest session to an account at any time using the same email address.
