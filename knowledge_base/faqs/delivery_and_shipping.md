# Delivery & Shipping — Frequently Asked Questions

_Last updated: June 1, 2026_

---

## Q1. How long does standard delivery take?

Standard delivery timelines depend on the destination zone:

| Zone | Estimated Delivery |
|---|---|
| Metro cities (Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad) | 3–5 business days |
| Tier-2 cities (Jaipur, Lucknow, Nagpur, Indore, Bhopal, Coimbatore, etc.) | 5–7 business days |
| Remote / rural / North-East pincodes | 7–12 business days |

Business days exclude Sundays and public holidays. Timelines begin from the moment the seller dispatches your order, which is typically within 1–2 days of order placement for ShopEasy-fulfilled items.

---

## Q2. Is express delivery available? What does it cost?

Yes. **ShopEasy Express** is available for select pincodes and product categories. Express delivery guarantees delivery within **1–2 business days** from dispatch and costs an additional **₹99 per order** on top of any standard shipping fee. You can check express availability on the product page or at checkout. If ShopEasy fails to deliver within the express window, the ₹99 express fee is refunded automatically within 24 hours.

---

## Q3. Do you deliver to all pincodes in India?

ShopEasy delivers to **26,000+ pincodes** across India. To check serviceability:
1. Enter your pincode on any product page under "Check Delivery".
2. Or enter it at checkout — if your pincode is unserviceable, you will be notified before payment.

For pincodes not served by private couriers (Delhivery, BlueDart), orders are routed through **India Post** which covers virtually all addresses but adds 3–5 extra business days. India Post delivery is shown as "Extended Delivery" at checkout.

## Q4. How do I track my order?

1. Go to **My Orders** in the app or website and tap your order.
2. The **Tracking** tab shows real-time status from the courier partner.
3. You will also receive SMS and WhatsApp updates at each stage: Packed → Dispatched → In Transit → Out for Delivery → Delivered.
4. If the order is shipped via Delhivery, you can also track directly at delhivery.com using the AWB number shown in your order details. For BlueDart, use bluedart.com. For India Post, use indiapost.gov.in.

## Q5. What happens if I miss the delivery attempt?

Our delivery partners make **up to 3 delivery attempts**:
- Attempt 1: On the scheduled day
- Attempt 2: Next business day (you receive an SMS/call notification)
- Attempt 3: One more business day after that

If all three attempts fail, the order is marked "Return to Origin" (RTO) and sent back to the seller. A full refund (for prepaid orders) or no charge (for COD) is processed. You may re-order the item once the RTO is confirmed. To avoid this, update your availability in the delivery notification SMS or contact support to reschedule.

## Q6. Why is a delivery OTP required?

For orders above **₹2,000** or for certain high-value categories (electronics, gold/silver jewellery), ShopEasy uses OTP-based delivery verification. A 6-digit OTP is sent to your registered mobile number at the time of delivery. You must share this OTP with the delivery executive to receive the package. This prevents unauthorised delivery and protects against fraud.

If you did not receive the OTP, ask the delivery executive to wait while you request a new OTP via the app (My Orders → Resend OTP). Do not share your OTP over the phone with anyone claiming to be from ShopEasy — our agents never ask for this.

## Q7. Which courier partners does ShopEasy use?

ShopEasy partners with multiple logistics providers:

| Courier | Coverage |
|---|---|
| **Delhivery** | Primary partner for most metro and tier-2 cities |
| **BlueDart** | Premium partner for express shipments |
| **Ekart Logistics** | Bengaluru region and select areas |
| **India Post** | Remote, rural, North-East, Andaman & Nicobar, Lakshadweep |
| **DTDC** | Supplementary for select South India pincodes |

The courier partner is assigned automatically based on pincode and cannot be chosen by the customer.

---

## Q8. Can I reschedule a delivery?

Yes, if you miss a delivery attempt:
1. Click the rescheduling link in the delivery attempt SMS.
2. Or go to **My Orders → Reschedule Delivery** and pick a date within the next 3 business days.
3. You can reschedule a maximum of **2 times per order**.

Note: Rescheduling is not available for India Post shipments — for those, you will need to collect the package from your nearest post office using the delivery notification slip and your Aadhaar or any photo ID.
