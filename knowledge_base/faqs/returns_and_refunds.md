# Returns & Refunds — Frequently Asked Questions

_Last updated: June 1, 2026_

---

## Q1. What is the return window on ShopEasy?

Most products sold on ShopEasy carry a **10-day return window** counted from the date of delivery. Electronics (mobiles, laptops, tablets, cameras, and accessories) have a shorter **7-day return window** due to rapid product cycles and manufacturer policies. Perishables, innerwear/lingerie, and customised or personalised items are **not eligible for return** under any circumstances. The return window begins on the day the item is marked "Delivered" in your order timeline.

---

## Q2. How do I initiate a return?

1. Log in to your ShopEasy account and go to **My Orders**.
2. Select the order and tap **Return / Replace**.
3. Choose a reason (defective, wrong item, not as described, change of mind, etc.).
4. Upload photos of the item and its packaging if prompted (mandatory for defective/wrong-item claims).
5. Select your preferred refund method (original payment source or ShopEasy Wallet).
6. A pickup slot will be offered within 1–2 business days at your registered address.

If you used guest checkout, initiate the return via the link in your order confirmation email or contact support with your order ID.

---

## Q3. How long does a refund take?

| Refund Destination | Timeline |
|---|---|
| UPI (same VPA) | 24–48 hours |
| Credit / Debit Card | 5–7 business days |
| Net Banking | 5–7 business days |
| ShopEasy Wallet | 2–3 business days |
| COD orders → Bank account (NEFT) | 5–7 business days |
| COD orders → ShopEasy Wallet | Instant on return verification |

Refunds are initiated only after the returned item is picked up and quality-checked at our fulfilment centre. Quality check typically takes 1 business day after pickup.

---

## Q4. What condition must the item be in for a return to be accepted?

The item must be:
- In its **original, unused condition** with all original packaging, tags, manuals, and accessories intact.
- Accompanied by the **original invoice** (you can download it from My Orders).
- Free from physical damage caused after delivery (scratches, broken screens, liquid damage).

For electronics, the **IMEI / serial number** on the device must match the one on the invoice. Any discrepancy will result in the return being rejected.

---

## Q5. Which items cannot be returned?

The following categories are **non-returnable**:

- **Innerwear, lingerie, swimwear, and socks** (hygiene reasons)
- **Customised / personalised items** (engraved jewellery, custom prints, monogrammed gifts)
- **Perishables** (fresh food, flowers, cakes)
- **Opened software / digital downloads / gift cards**
- **Consumables** once opened (health supplements, cosmetics if seal broken)
- Items explicitly marked "Non-Returnable" on the product page at time of purchase

---

## Q6. Will I get a partial refund or full refund?

| Return Reason | Refund Amount |
|---|---|
| Defective / damaged product | 100% of order value |
| Wrong item received | 100% + ₹50 courtesy coupon |
| Item not as described | 100% of order value |
| Change of mind (item >₹999) | Full minus ₹75 restocking fee |
| Change of mind (item ≤₹999) | 100% of order value |

Original shipping charges (if any) are refunded only if the return is due to ShopEasy's error (defective, wrong item, or not as described).

---

## Q7. How does the COD refund process work?

For Cash on Delivery orders, refunds cannot be reversed to cash at the doorstep. Instead:

1. When you initiate a return, you will be asked to provide your **bank account details (account number + IFSC code)** or choose **ShopEasy Wallet**.
2. If you choose bank transfer (NEFT), the refund is processed within **5–7 business days** after the return is verified.
3. If you choose ShopEasy Wallet, the amount is credited **instantly** upon return verification and can be used for future purchases or transferred to your bank account.

---

## Q8. What happens if my return is rejected after pickup?

If the quality-check team finds the returned item does not meet return eligibility criteria (damaged by customer, missing accessories, IMEI mismatch, etc.), the item will be **shipped back to you at no charge** within 3–5 business days and the return request will be closed. You may contact support within 48 hours of receiving the re-shipped item if you believe the rejection was in error, and a senior agent will review it.
