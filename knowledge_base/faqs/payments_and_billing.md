## Q2. My UPI payment was deducted but the order was not placed. What do I do?

This is a known issue that can occur when the UPI transaction is processed by your bank but the confirmation does not reach ShopEasy in time. Here is what happens automatically:

1. ShopEasy's payment reconciliation system detects the deducted amount within **1–2 hours**.
2. A refund is initiated to the **same UPI VPA (address)** from which the payment was made.
3. The refund reaches your bank account within **24–48 hours** of initiation.

You do not need to take any action. However, if you have not received the refund after 48 hours:
- Contact support with your **UPI transaction reference number (UTR)** and the approximate time of payment.
- Our finance team will manually verify and process the refund within 1 business day.

Do not re-attempt the payment immediately — wait 30 minutes and check your order status first. If no order was created, the money will be refunded.

## Q3. Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available for my order?

COD is available subject to the following conditions:

- **Order value**: Must be under **₹5,000** (inclusive of all items and charges).
- **Pincode**: Your delivery pincode must be COD-serviceable. Some remote pincodes only support prepaid orders.
- **Product category**: High-value electronics (laptops, smartphones above ₹15,000), gold/silver jewellery, and certain seller-fulfilled items may not be COD-eligible.
- **Account standing**: Customers with a high rate of COD order cancellations may temporarily lose COD access.

A **COD handling fee of ₹30** is charged per order. This fee is non-refundable unless the order is cancelled before shipment.

## Q4. How do I download my order invoice / GST invoice?

1. Go to **My Orders** and select the relevant order.
2. Tap **Invoice** → **Download PDF**.
3. The invoice includes itemised GST breakdowns (CGST + SGST for intra-state, IGST for inter-state) as required under the GST Act.

For B2B buyers who need invoices with their GSTIN:
- Add your GSTIN under **My Account → Business Profile** before placing the order.
- GST invoices with your GSTIN cannot be generated after the order is delivered.

## Q5. Is GST charged on my order?

Yes. GST is included in the price displayed on ShopEasy product pages (MRP-inclusive pricing). The GST rate varies by product category:

| Category | GST Rate |
|---|---|
| Groceries / staples | 0–5% |
| Clothing & footwear (≤₹1,000) | 5% |
| Clothing & footwear (>₹1,000) | 12% |
| Electronics | 18% |
| Luxury / cosmetics | 28% |

ShopEasy is GST-registered under GSTIN **27AADCS1234B1Z5** (Maharashtra). Your invoice will reflect the applicable GSTIN of the seller for marketplace orders.

## Q6. How secure are my payment details on ShopEasy?

ShopEasy does **not store** your full card number, CVV, or UPI PIN at any point. All card transactions are processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant payment gateways (Razorpay / PayU). 3D Secure (OTP-based authentication) is mandatory for all card payments. UPI payments are processed via NPCI's UPI infrastructure, which is regulated by the Reserve Bank of India.

If you ever receive a call or message asking for your ShopEasy password, card OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV claiming to be from ShopEasy, it is a scam — report it immediately to support and to your bank.
